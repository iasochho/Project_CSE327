**Project Description**

With the increasing awareness of health and fitness, many individuals are turning to digital tools to help them manage workouts, monitor progress, and maintain consistency. Mobile fitness applications have become essential tools for gym enthusiasts, athletes, and beginners who want structured workout tracking and personalized fitness guidance.
This project aims to develop an AI-powered fitness tracking mobile application that allows users to log workouts, monitor progress, receive personalized recommendations, and interact with an AI chatbot for fitness guidance. The system will combine traditional workout tracking features with artificial intelligence assistance to improve user engagement and training efficiency.
The application will be inspired by popular workout tracking apps such as Strong Workout Tracker Gym Log and Hevy Workout Tracker, which allow users to log sets, reps, and weights. However, our system will extend these capabilities by integrating an AI chatbot that can answer fitness questions, recommend workouts, and assist users in planning their training routines.
The main objective of this project is to provide a smart and interactive fitness management platform that helps users stay consistent with their training while also learning about proper workout techniques, recovery strategies, and fitness planning.
